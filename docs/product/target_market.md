# CashPulse Target Market

## 1. Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)
### 1.1 Company Attributes
- Size: 3-50 employees.
- Geography: Israel-first.
- Revenue profile: recurring and/or project-based invoicing.
- Operational maturity: uses invoices but collections remain manual.

### 1.2 Operational Signals
- Tracks receivables in Excel/Google Sheets.
- Uses WhatsApp as primary client communication channel.
- Experiences repeated payment delays and unstructured follow-ups.

## 2. Segment Breakdown
### 2.1 Service Agencies
- Many concurrent clients and monthly retainers.
- Pain: high reminder overhead and poor visibility.

### 2.2 Consultants and Freelancers
- Limited back-office resources.
- Pain: collections compete with delivery work.

### 2.3 Software Companies (Small)
- Mixed payment schedules.
- Pain: clients defer payment despite active service usage.

### 2.4 Contractors and Field Services
- High dependency on milestone payments.
- Pain: delayed payments impact payroll and supplier obligations.

## 3. Buyer and User Roles
### 3.1 Economic Buyer
- Owner/founder or finance manager responsible for cash outcomes.

### 3.2 Daily User
- Office admin, operations coordinator, or owner.

### 3.3 Influencer
- External accountant/bookkeeper advising process improvements.

## 4. Jobs To Be Done
1. "When invoices are unpaid, help me identify who to contact first so I can collect faster."
2. "When I send reminders, let me do it quickly in the channel my clients actually respond to."
3. "When a client pays, update status automatically so I don't waste time checking manually."
4. "When planning upcoming expenses, show me realistic expected incoming cash dates."

## 5. Adoption Barriers
- Confusion with accounting software category.
- Concern about setup time.
- Fear of losing existing spreadsheet workflows.

## 6. Go-To-Market Messaging Priorities
- "Built for collections, not accounting complexity."
- "Send WhatsApp reminders in seconds."
- "Know exactly who owes you and when cash is expected."

## 7. Market Exclusions (for MVP)
- Large enterprises requiring complex role hierarchies.
- Businesses needing bank-level treasury integration from day one.
- Companies needing full ERP/accounting migration.

## 8. Localization Requirements for Israeli SMBs
- Support Israeli phone number formatting and validation.
- Support ILS currency formatting by default.
- Provide templates that align with Hebrew/WhatsApp business communication styles.
