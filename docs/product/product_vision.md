# CashPulse Product Vision

## 1. Vision Statement
CashPulse will become the default receivables operating layer for Israeli SMBs: a lightweight, action-oriented platform that converts unpaid invoices into predictable cash inflow with minimal operational overhead.

## 2. Strategic Intent
### 2.1 Near-Term (MVP Horizon)
- Replace spreadsheet-and-chat collection processes with a structured workflow.
- Drive measurable reduction in overdue receivables.
- Establish trust through transparent payment status and reliable reminders.

### 2.2 Mid-Term (Post-MVP)
- Build predictive collections intelligence using payment behavior signals.
- Add automation policies (auto-reminders by segment/risk).
- Expand from reactive reminders to proactive cash planning.

### 2.3 Long-Term
- Become a collections intelligence system of record for SMBs, while still integrating with external invoicing/accounting tools rather than replacing them.

## 3. Product North Star
**North Star Metric:** Collected amount within 14 days of due date.

Supporting indicators:
- % debts paid after first reminder
- Median overdue days
- Active companies sending reminders weekly
- Debt-to-payment closure rate via webhook automation

## 4. Product Positioning
### 4.1 Positioning Statement
For Israeli SMBs struggling with delayed customer payments, CashPulse is a collections-focused SaaS that helps teams identify open debts, trigger WhatsApp reminders, collect through instant links, and track status in real time.

### 4.2 Competitive Differentiators
- WhatsApp-native collection workflow.
- Action-first UI designed for speed.
- Strong boundary against accounting complexity.
- AI predictions tuned to collection use cases.

## 5. Guiding Experience Principles
1. **Immediate actionability:** every screen should expose the next best action.
2. **Operational confidence:** every debt has clear current status and history.
3. **Low-friction communication:** reminders should be sendable in one to two clicks.
4. **Explainable intelligence:** AI outputs must be understandable and editable.

## 6. Strategic Constraints
- Avoid feature creep into full finance suite territory.
- Preserve simple onboarding for non-technical SMB users.
- Keep data model extensible without introducing enterprise complexity.

## 7. Product Evolution Themes
### 7.1 Collections Workflow Maturity
- Phase 1: manual reminders + webhook closure.
- Phase 2: cadence automation + queue prioritization.
- Phase 3: AI-assisted action recommendations.

### 7.2 Data Intelligence Maturity
- Phase 1: deterministic status and overdue calculations.
- Phase 2: reliability scoring and expected payment prediction.
- Phase 3: cohort and seasonality-aware forecasts.

## 8. Trust and Reliability Vision
CashPulse must be viewed by SMB users as an operationally critical tool. This requires:
- Deterministic status transitions.
- No silent payment failures.
- Full audit log for reminders, webhook events, and manual overrides.

## 9. Vision to Execution Alignment
Each roadmap item must satisfy at least one of:
- Increase collection speed.
- Increase collection certainty.
- Reduce manual collection workload.

If an item does not satisfy one of these, it should be deprioritized.
